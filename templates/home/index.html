Mix, Find, Discover - Discover a world of Elements waiting to be found

Your mission

After a successful evacuation from Earth, our heroes aboard spacecraft "MARS SHIP" head to the red planet. To prepare the planet for colonization, they created a generator of elements. Now they must create as many items as possible. Explore, mix, invent! Every attempt brings us closer to salvation!

The four basic elements underlie everything: wind, earth, fire and water. By combining them in the Cauldron you create something new.

When a player discovers a new element, they will unlock that element along with a reward, the reward will contain the elemnent they found and a certain amount of credits based on the element they found.

Each time the player uses the Cauldron it will cost a fee of 15 Credits. These credits can be used to purchase any of the four basic elements in the shop.
